Before your visit

A note on symptoms before you come in.

If you're feeling unwell, please reschedule.

Because we care about the well-being of our patients and staff, we take extra precautions by disinfecting exam rooms and waiting areas frequently.

We ask that any patient who has — or is in close contact with someone who has — any of the following symptoms reschedule their appointment:

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Sore throat
  • Shortness of breath

Call 720-550-6175 or email info@boulderbiologics.com and we'll move your appointment without a fee.
